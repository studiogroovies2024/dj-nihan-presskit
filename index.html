About DJ Nihan

Dynamic DJ from Nepal, known for electrifying performances and versatile music selection. A career spanning since 2016 has established him as a prominent figure in the Nepali music scene.

He captivates audiences with his unique blend of open-format DJing — reading the room, bridging genres and keeping the floor moving from first drop to last.

03

Career Highlights

Resident DJ at prestigious venues like the Dusit Princess and the Marriott Hotel, and notably the only pioneer DJ instructor in Nepal — sharing his craft with the next generation behind the decks.

His sets have powered floors well beyond home turf — from the Ibiza Pool parties of Thailand to Gravity and Error across India, and headline nights at Senate Club, Club Nexus, Club Platinum, Club 10X, Catwalk and Club 16. Across Nepal he’s commanded the booth at landmark rooms including the Hotel Yak & Yeti, Hotel Annapurna and the Hyatt Palace — and many more.
