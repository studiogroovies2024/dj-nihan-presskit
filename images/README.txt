DJ NIHAN — PRESS KIT / IMAGE DROP-IN GUIDE
==========================================

Drop these files into this folder (images/). Until they exist, the site shows a
styled red-hatched placeholder with a label — nothing breaks.

Use the EXACT filenames below.

  hero.jpg     Main artist photo. B&W portrait, leather jacket, moody / high
               contrast. Portrait orientation, ~1200 x 1500 px.

  event-1.jpg  Live photo — wide crowd / main-stage shot. ~1200 x 900 px.
  event-2.jpg  Live photo — DJ at the decks, hands on the mixer. ~800 x 900 px.
  event-3.jpg  Live photo — crowd, hands up under stage lights. ~800 x 900 px.
  event-4.jpg  Live photo — DJ silhouette against stage lighting. ~800 x 900 px.
  event-5.jpg  Live photo — wide club atmosphere / lasers. ~1200 x 900 px.

  og.jpg       (optional) Social share preview image, ~1200 x 630 px.
               Used for link previews on WhatsApp / Instagram / Facebook.

TIPS
- JPG or WebP both work. For web, export at ~70-80% quality to keep it fast.
- The hero photo is automatically rendered in grayscale by the site's CSS,
  so a colour photo is fine — it will display black & white.
- Keep the aspect ratios close to the suggestions so nothing gets awkwardly
  cropped (images are cover-fitted).
